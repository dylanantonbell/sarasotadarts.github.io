About

Sarasota Darters use this group and website to promote and centralize dart activity across Sarasota County. We post active blind draws, dart league schedules, and use polls and announcements to build awareness. Please contact an admin if you know of a bar running a blind draw or league that’s not listed here.

🏆 Steel Tip Leagues

  • MDLS (Monday Dart League Sarasota): Traveling steel-tip league held every Monday at local bars.
  • TDLS (Tuesday Dart League Sarasota): Traveling steel-tip league held every Tuesday night at rotating venues.

🏆 Soft Tip Leagues

Any location with connected BullShooter boards may run soft tip leagues.

Current venues include:

  • The American Legion Post 266 (Richardson)

  • Moondoggy's Saloon
  • Fanatics
  • The Last Call

📌 Group Rules

  • Be Kind and Courteous: We're all here to create a welcoming community. Respect others and keep debate healthy.
  • No Personal Attacks: Negative posts about league bars or venues will be removed unless they concern public safety.
  • Support Growth: We're working to foster more dart venues and greater league involvement in Sarasota County.
