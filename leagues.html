Local Dart Leagues

League information, standings, champions, sponsor notes, and state team records for Sarasota area darts.

TDLS

Tuesday Dart League Sarasota (TDLS) is our flagship steel-tip league held every Tuesday at various bars around Sarasota. Players compete in singles and doubles across multiple formats. Seasonal schedules and standings are updated weekly.

Sponsor: Man Cave Warehouse

MDLS

Monday Dart League Sarasota (MDLS) runs on Monday nights and offers a more casual steel-tip league for newcomers and veterans alike. Great for sharpening skills in a team format.

Soft Tip Leagues

Sarasota hosts several soft-tip dart leagues at bars equipped with BullShooter and Arachnid boards. These leagues offer automatic scoring and cater to both new players and experienced competitors.

Stay tuned for sign-up info and seasonal announcements!
