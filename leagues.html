Tuesday Dart League Sarasota (TDLS)

TDLS is our flagship steel-tip league held every Tuesday at various bars around Sarasota. Players compete in singles and doubles across multiple formats. Seasonal schedules and standings are updated weekly.

Monday Dart League Sarasota (MDLS)

MDLS runs on Monday nights and offers a more casual steel-tip league for newcomers and veterans alike. Great for sharpening skills in a team format.

Soft Tip Dart Leagues

There are several soft-tip leagues across Sarasota at bars equipped with soft-tip boards. Stay tuned for listings and sign-up information.
