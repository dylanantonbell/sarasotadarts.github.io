Sarasota Darts is a central hub for dart players and enthusiasts in the Sarasota area. This website shares information about local dart leagues, active blind draws, tournament results, venue listings, and dart-related events.

We aim to help players find where to play, when to compete, and how to get involved. If you have questions, need more info, or would like to suggest updates, you can reach out directly via our public Facebook group.

Much of the community coordination, event posts, and blind draw discussions happen in that group. Polls and posts there help gather attention to what's active in Sarasota County.

This site was created and maintained by Dylan Bell (TDLS President) and will continue to be updated as new content becomes available.
